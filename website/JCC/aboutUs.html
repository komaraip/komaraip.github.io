About Us

Jockey's Conference Center

"A House Where Ideas Meet Reality"

Jockey's Conference Center is a modern venue in Jakarta that provides exceptional facilities and services for meetings, conferences, and events. Our team works closely with clients to ensure every event is successful. We are conveniently located and committed to providing a space where ideas come to life.

History

Founded with a Vision for Excellence

2016

Jockey's Conference Center was founded by a team of entrepreneurs who shared a passion for creating a premier venue for meetings and events in Jakarta.

Expansion and Sustainability

2017

Jockey's Conference Center expanded its facilities with the addition of a new hall, increasing its capacity and allowing it to host even larger events. The expansion also enabled the center to incorporate environmentally sustainable practices into its operations.

Go International

2018

Jockey's Conference Center hosted its first international conference, bringing together experts from around the world to discuss emerging trends and share insights on the latest developments in their fields.

Customized Services and Exceptional Experiences

2020

Jockey's Conference Center continued to attract clients from a diverse range of industries, including finance, technology, and hospitality. Its reputation for customized services, state-of-the-art facilities, and exceptional experiences solidified its position as one of the leading conference centers in Jakarta.

Certificate

ISO 22000:2018

Jockey's Conference Center is proud to announce that we have passed an in-depth set of assessments to achieve the honor of ISO 22000-2018. The implementation of ISO 22000-2018 allows us to constantly improve our quality management systems and the processes of our organization. This certification shows that we are truly committed to the quality of our ingredients, as part of our business culture and philosophy. The certificate is officially received by our General Manager, Mr. Komara Indra Putra.
